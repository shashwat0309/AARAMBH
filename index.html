KNOW ABOUT US

Medicaps Innovation and Incubation Centre (MIIC) by Medicaps University is a platform for nurturing and overseeing innovation and entrepreneurship. We nurture and mentor aspiring entrepreneur and transform their innovative ideas into viable entrepreneurial initiatives through excellence and service. No idea is ever too small, and no problem is ever too big for us. MIIC provides opportunity for budding entrepreneur to take their startups to next level. Young minds transforms their innovative ideas into viable business propositions.

ABOUT OUR UNIVERSITY

Medi-Caps University is one of the brand names in the arena of technical education and is contributing in making Indore an educational hub. We have an optimal blend of academic brilliance and industry exposure, which is supplemented by highly specialized visiting faculty and industry experts, senior professionals from various segments of different industry/ business houses. We produce conceptually sound, practically oriented, value driven and well groomed technocrats and professionals. The highly motivated youngsters on the campus are a constant source of pride.

Why We Are Organising

Business incubators and accelerators nurture the talent and ventures that form the backbone of the knowledge-driven economies of the 21st century. Based on this understanding, the MIIC 2020 provides incubator and accelerator managers, corporate innovation officers, and policymakers a platform to connect and learn about the best ways to nurture innovation in their respective ecosystems. It will provide corporate innovation managers to build network in the growing tech community and collaborate with some of the finest minds.

FAQs For Hackathon

Boost Your Knowledge

Q. What is Code Tussle?

A. Code Tussle is a complete online Hackathon conducted by Medi-Caps Innovation and Incubation Center (MIIC) and Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell). It is one of the most important activities of AARAMBH-2020.

Q. When will Code Tussle start?

A. On September 20th, at 12:00 noon, Code Tussle will commence on GitHub. It is a 36-hour hackathon and will complete at 11:00 PM on September 21st, 2020. After which judges and organizers will evaluate your submissions and declare results on the website after the conclusion of the event.

Q. What is the procedure of Code Tussle?

A. Procedure:
1. Make a team according to the above-said team size and decide your leader.
2. Register yourself here (link)
3. Choose a theme for your problem statement here (Link)
4. Code Tussle starts. Provide bank account details of your leader for transferring of prize money in case your team wins.
5. Make your product (Android app, Website)
6. Submit it for evaluation on GitHub.
7. Results will be declared on the website

Q. How to make the submission on GitHub?

A. Update your commits on GitHub from time to time and prepare a video presentation of your final product.

Q. How will you check for Plagiarism?

A. We have experts for checking plagiarism and GitHub provides support in the same along with various tools.

Q. When will the result be announced?

A. Not later than a week after completion of Code Tussle.
